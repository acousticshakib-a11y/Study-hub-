Mijbil the Otter

Easy Summary

This chapter is about the author's pet otter named Mijbil. The author bought Mijbil from Iraq and took care of him. Mijbil was playful, intelligent and loved water. The chapter describes the funny and interesting experiences of the author with his pet otter.
