Application Writing

HSLC Class 10 Important Applications

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Universal Application Writing Formula

Important Note: Learn this format carefully. By changing only a few lines, you can write most applications asked in the HSLC Examination.

Formula:

To,
The Headmaster,
____________ School,
____________.

Subject: Prayer for __________________.

Sir,

I beg to state that ________________________________________________.

Therefore, I request you kindly to ________________________________.

For this act of kindness, I shall remain grateful to you.

Yours obediently,
Your Name
Class X

Can Be Used For:

✓ Leave of Absence
✓ Sick Leave
✓ School Leaving Certificate
✓ Fee Concession
✓ Educational Tour
✓ Library Facilities
✓ Common Room
✓ Tree Plantation Programme

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT APPLICATION

4. Application for Fee Concession

Question: Write an application to the Headmaster requesting a fee concession.

Answer:

To,
The Headmaster,
ABC High School, Dhubri.

Subject: Prayer for fee concession.

Sir,

I beg to state that I am a student of Class X of your school. My father is a poor farmer and he finds it difficult to pay my school fees regularly. I have always been regular in my studies and have performed well in the examinations. Therefore, I request you kindly to grant me a fee concession. For this act of kindness, I shall remain grateful to you.

Yours obediently,
Shakib Hassan
Class X

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT APPLICATION

5. Application for Educational Tour

Question: Write an application to the Headmaster requesting permission to join an Educational Tour.

Answer:

To,
The Headmaster,
ABC High School, Dhubri.

Subject: Prayer for permission to join an Educational Tour.

Sir,

I beg to state that our school is going to organise an Educational Tour. I am interested in joining the tour as it will help me gain knowledge and experience. Therefore, I request you kindly to allow me to take part in the Educational Tour. For this act of kindness, I shall remain grateful to you.

Yours obediently,
Shakib Hassan
Class X

⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT APPLICATION

6. Application for Library Facilities

Question: Write an application to the Headmaster requesting him to improve the library facilities of your school.

Answer:

To,
The Headmaster,
ABC High School, Dhubri.

Subject: Prayer for improving library facilities.

Sir,

I beg to state that our school library does not have enough books for the students. Many useful books and reference books are not available. A well-equipped library helps students gain knowledge and improve their studies. Therefore, I request you kindly to improve the library facilities by adding more books. For this act of kindness, I shall remain grateful to you.

Yours obediently,
Shakib Hassan
Class X

⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT APPLICATION

7. Application for Setting Up a Common Room

Question: Write an application to the Headmaster requesting him to set up a common room in your school.

Answer:

To,
The Headmaster,
ABC High School, Dhubri.

Subject: Prayer for setting up a common room.

Sir,

I beg to state that there is no common room in our school. During free periods, students have no proper place to sit and read newspapers or magazines. A common room will be useful for the students. Therefore, I request you kindly to take necessary steps to set up a common room in our school. For this act of kindness, I shall remain grateful to you.

Yours obediently,
Shakib Hassan
Class X

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT APPLICATION

1. Application for Leave of Absence

Question: Write an application to the Headmaster requesting leave of absence for three days due to urgent family work.

Answer:

To,
The Headmaster,
ABC High School, Dhubri.

Subject: Prayer for leave of absence for three days.

Sir,

I beg to state that I am a student of Class X of your school. Due to some urgent family work, I shall not be able to attend school for three days. Therefore, I request you kindly to grant me leave from 10 June to 12 June 2026. I shall be highly grateful for your kind consideration.

Yours obediently,
Shakib Hassan
Class X

Note: This application can also be used for family function, marriage ceremony, personal work and other valid reasons.
