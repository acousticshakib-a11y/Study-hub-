Question 56

Describe the character of Lencho.

Answer

Lencho was a poor farmer who lived with his family. He was hardworking, honest and simple. He had great faith in God and believed that God would always help him. Even after losing his crops in the hailstorm, he did not lose hope. His strong faith makes him one of the most memorable characters in the story.

Question 57

How did the postmaster help Lencho?

Answer

The postmaster was touched by Lencho's faith in God. He decided to help him by collecting money from his colleagues and giving some money from his own salary. He sent the money to Lencho in an envelope signed by God. His kindness and generosity helped Lencho during a difficult time.

Question 58

What happened after the hailstorm? Describe briefly.

Answer

After the hailstorm, all of Lencho's crops were destroyed. The fields looked white as if covered with salt. Lencho became worried because he had no food or money left for his family. However, he did not lose faith and decided to write a letter to God asking for help.

Question 59

Why is the story called 'A Letter to God'?

Answer

The story is called 'A Letter to God' because the main event of the story is Lencho writing a letter to God. He believed that God would help him after his crops were destroyed. The story shows his faith and also the kindness of the postmaster who tried to answer his prayer.

Question 60

What is the main message of the story 'A Letter to God'?

Answer

The story teaches us the importance of faith, hope and kindness. Lencho's faith in God gave him strength during a difficult time. The postmaster's generosity shows that helping others is a noble act. The story reminds us to remain hopeful and support people in need.
