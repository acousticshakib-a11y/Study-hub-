Notice Writing

HSLC Class 10 Important Notice Writing Questions & Answers

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Universal Notice Writing Formula

Learn this format carefully. By changing only a few details, you can write most notices asked in HSLC Examination.

ABC HIGH SCHOOL, DHUBRI
NOTICE

Date: _______________

Subject: __________________

All the students are hereby informed that _____________________________. Interested students are requested to _______________________________. For further details, contact the undersigned.

Signature
XYZ
Secretary / Head Boy / Head Girl

1. Notice for Tree Plantation Programme

Question: Write a notice informing the students about a Tree Plantation Programme.

Answer:

NOTICE

Date: 1 June 2026

Tree Plantation Programme

All students are informed that a Tree Plantation Programme will be held in our school on 5 June 2026 at 10:00 a.m. Students are requested to take part in the programme and bring one sapling.

Shakib Hassan
Secretary, Eco Club
