1. Letter to a Friend Inviting Him to Spend a Few Days at Your Home

Question: Write a letter to your friend inviting him to spend a few days at your home during the summer vacation.

Answer:

Dhubri, Assam
15 June 2026

Dear Rahim,

I hope you are fine and in good health. I am also fine here. Our summer vacation has started. I would like to invite you to spend a few days at my home during this vacation. We shall spend our time happily together. We will play games, visit nearby places and enjoy our holidays. Please come to my home next week. I am eagerly waiting for your visit. Convey my regards to your parents.

Yours lovingly,
Shakib

Most Important Universal Friend Letter

Question: Write a letter to your friend on any suitable topic such as congratulating him on success, wishing him good luck, describing your school, inviting him to your home, or sharing any important information.

Note: Learn this format carefully. By changing only a few lines in the body, you can write most letters asked in the HSLC Examination.

Answer:

Dhubri, Assam
15 June 2026

Dear Rahim,

I hope you are fine and in good health. I am also fine here. I am writing this letter to inform you about ____________________________. _________________________________________________ _________________________________________________ _________________________________________________ I hope you will be happy to know this. Please write back to me soon. Convey my regards to your parents.

Yours lovingly,
Shakib

How to Use This Letter

For Congratulation: Write about your friend's success.

For Good Luck: Write about the upcoming examination and wish him success.

For School Description: Write about your school, teachers and facilities.

For Invitation: Invite your friend to visit your home or attend a function.

For Any Information: Write the necessary details in the blank space.
