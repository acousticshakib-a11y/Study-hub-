Madam Rides the Bus

Easy Summary

Madam Rides the Bus is the story of an eight-year-old girl named Valli. She was curious and wanted to ride a bus alone. She carefully planned her journey and travelled to the town. During the journey, she learned many things and returned home safely.

2 Marks Questions and Answers

Q1. Why was Valli eager to ride the bus?
Answer: Valli was curious about the outside world. She wanted to experience a bus journey alone and see the town.

Q2. How did Valli collect money for her bus journey?
Answer: Valli saved every coin she received and avoided spending money on toys and treats.

Q3. Why did the conductor call Valli 'Madam'?
Answer: The conductor called her 'Madam' because she behaved confidently and like a grown-up person.

Q4. What was Valli's favourite pastime?
Answer: Her favourite pastime was standing at the front door and watching the bus and the people travelling in it.

Q5. What information did Valli gather about the bus journey?
Answer: She learned the fare, distance, travel time and bus schedule by listening to people's conversations.

Q6. How far was the town from Valli's village?
Answer: The town was six miles away from Valli's village.

Q7. Why did Valli not accept the conductor's help?
Answer: Valli wanted to show that she was independent and capable of travelling alone.

Q8. What did Valli enjoy during the journey?
Answer: She enjoyed watching the green fields, trees, canals and other beautiful sights outside the bus.

Q9. What made Valli laugh during the bus ride?
Answer: A young cow running in front of the bus made Valli laugh happily.

Q10. Why did Valli not get off the bus in town?
Answer: She had planned only a bus ride and wanted to return home by the same bus.

Q11. What did the conductor offer Valli in the town?
Answer: The conductor offered her a cold drink, but she politely refused.

Q12. Why did Valli refuse the cold drink?
Answer: She wanted to save her money for the return journey and had no extra money to spend.

Q13. What sad sight did Valli see on her return journey?
Answer: She saw the same cow lying dead by the roadside after being hit by a vehicle.

Q14. How did the dead cow affect Valli?
Answer: It made her sad and thoughtful, and she lost interest in enjoying the journey.

Q15. What does Valli's character reveal about her?
Answer: Valli was curious, intelligent, confident and determined.

Q16. Why was the bus journey important for Valli?
Answer: It fulfilled her dream and gave her a new experience of the outside world.

Q17. How did Valli plan her journey carefully?
Answer: She collected information about the bus timings, fare and route before travelling.

Q18. What lesson did Valli learn from the journey?
Answer: She learned that life has both joyful and sad experiences.

Q19. Why did Valli keep her trip a secret?
Answer: She feared that her mother would not allow her to travel alone.

Q20. What is the main theme of the story?
Answer: The story highlights curiosity, independence, courage and learning through experience.

3 to 5 Marks Questions and Answers

Q1. Describe the character of Valli.
Answer: Valli was an eight-year-old girl who was curious, intelligent and confident. She was determined to fulfil her dream of travelling by bus. She carefully collected information about the journey and saved money for it. Her courage and independence make her a memorable character.

Q2. How did Valli plan and prepare for her bus journey?
Answer: Valli carefully listened to conversations about the bus service. She learned the fare, distance and travel time. She saved sixty paise by avoiding unnecessary expenses. After gathering all the information, she planned her journey and travelled alone.

Q3. Describe Valli's first bus journey.
Answer: Valli's first bus journey was exciting and enjoyable. She sat in the bus and admired the beautiful scenery outside. She laughed at the sight of a young cow running in front of the bus. The journey fulfilled her dream and gave her a new experience of the outside world.

Q4. Why did the conductor call Valli 'Madam'?
Answer: The conductor called Valli 'Madam' because she behaved like an adult. She spoke confidently and refused unnecessary help. Her mature behaviour amused the conductor, who jokingly addressed her as 'Madam' throughout the journey.

Q5. What was Valli's reaction to the dead cow?
Answer: On her return journey, Valli saw the same cow that had made her laugh earlier lying dead on the roadside. The sight shocked and saddened her. She realised that life contains both happiness and sorrow, and she became quiet and thoughtful.

Q6. What does the story 'Madam Rides the Bus' teach us?
Answer: The story teaches us the importance of curiosity, courage and self-confidence. It also shows that experience is a great teacher. Through her journey, Valli learns valuable lessons about life and the world around her.

Q7. Why was the bus journey important for Valli?
Answer: The bus journey was important because it fulfilled Valli's long-cherished dream. It allowed her to explore the outside world independently. The experience increased her confidence and helped her understand life better.

Q8. How did Valli show determination and independence?
Answer: Valli showed determination by carefully planning her trip and saving money for it. She travelled alone without depending on anyone. She refused help from others and confidently managed the entire journey by herself.

Q9. Describe the relationship between Valli and the bus conductor.
Answer: The bus conductor was kind, friendly and helpful towards Valli. He enjoyed talking to her and called her 'Madam'. Valli also responded politely. Their friendly conversation made the journey pleasant and enjoyable.

Q10. Give a brief summary of 'Madam Rides the Bus'.
Answer: 'Madam Rides the Bus' is the story of an eight-year-old girl named Valli. She dreams of travelling by bus and carefully plans her journey. She enjoys the trip, observes the beauty of nature and gains new experiences. The story highlights curiosity, courage and learning through experience.
