My School

Question: Write an essay on "My School".

Answer:

The name of my school is ABC High School. It is one of the best schools in our area. It is situated in a peaceful environment. There are many classrooms, a library, a science laboratory and a large playground in our school. The teachers are qualified and friendly. They teach us with great care and help us in our studies. Many cultural and sports activities are organised in our school. We learn discipline, honesty and good manners here. I am proud of my school and love it very much.

Science in Everyday Life

Question: Write an essay on "Science in Everyday Life".

Answer:

Science plays an important role in our daily life. It has made our life easy, comfortable and fast. We use many scientific inventions such as mobile phones, computers, televisions and electric lights every day. Science has improved communication, transportation and medical treatment. Farmers also use scientific methods and machines in agriculture. Students use computers and the internet for learning. Science is a great blessing to mankind. We should use it wisely for the welfare of society.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT ESSAY

Discipline

Question: Write an essay on "Discipline".

Answer:

Discipline means obeying rules and maintaining order in life. It is very important for success. A disciplined person is punctual, honest and responsible. Students need discipline to study properly and achieve good results. Discipline helps us develop good habits and good character. It teaches us respect for others and makes society peaceful. Without discipline, life becomes disorderly. Therefore, everyone should practise discipline in daily life.

A Rainy Day

Question: Write an essay on "A Rainy Day".

Answer:

A rainy day is a day when it rains continuously. It is a common scene during the rainy season. Dark clouds cover the sky and heavy rain falls. Roads become muddy and water collects in low areas. People use umbrellas and raincoats while going outside. Farmers are happy because rain is necessary for crops. Trees and plants look fresh and green after rainfall. Although a rainy day causes some inconvenience, it is very useful for nature and agriculture.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT ESSAY

My Hobby

Question: Write an essay on "My Hobby".

Answer:

A hobby is an activity that we enjoy in our free time. My hobby is reading books. I read story books, biographies and magazines whenever I get time. Reading books increases my knowledge and improves my language skills. It also helps me learn new ideas and information. Books are our best friends because they guide us in life. My hobby makes my free time useful and enjoyable. I hope to continue it throughout my life.

My Favourite Teacher

Question: Write an essay on "My Favourite Teacher".

Answer:

My favourite teacher is Mr. Rahman. He teaches us English. He is kind, honest and hardworking. He explains every lesson in a simple and interesting way. He always encourages students to work hard and become good human beings. He is punctual and disciplined. All the students respect and love him. He helps weak students and guides us in our studies. I am proud to be his student. He is a source of inspiration for me.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ ASSAM SPECIAL ESSAY

Zubeen Garg

Question: Write an essay on "Zubeen Garg".

Answer:

Zubeen Garg is one of the most popular singers of Assam. He is also a music director, songwriter and actor. He was born on 18 November 1972 in Jorhat, Assam. His real name is Zubeen Borthakur. He has sung many beautiful Assamese, Hindi and Bengali songs. Zubeen Garg has made a great contribution to Assamese music and culture. His songs are loved by people of all ages. He is one of the most respected artists of Assam. I like his songs very much and admire him for his talent and dedication.
