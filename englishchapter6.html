The Hundred Dresses - II

Easy Summary

After Wanda left the school, Maddie and Peggy felt sorry for their behaviour. They realized that they had hurt Wanda's feelings. Maddie was especially upset because she had never tried to stop Peggy from teasing Wanda. She wanted to apologize to Wanda. The girls visited Wanda's house, but they found that her family had moved away. Later, Wanda sent a friendly letter to her teacher. She forgave Peggy and Maddie and gifted them her beautiful drawings. The story teaches us the importance of kindness, forgiveness and standing against bullying.

অসমীয়াত সহজ Summary

ৱাণ্ডা স্কুল এৰি যোৱাৰ পিছত মেড্ডী আৰু পেগীয়ে নিজৰ ভুল বুজি পায়। তেওঁলোকে অনুভৱ কৰে যে তেওঁলোকে ৱাণ্ডাৰ মনত আঘাত দিছিল। মেড্ডীয়ে বিশেষকৈ অনুতাপ কৰিছিল কাৰণ তাই কেতিয়াও ৱাণ্ডাক সমৰ্থন কৰা নাছিল। পিছত তেওঁলোকে ৱাণ্ডাৰ ঘৰলৈ গৈ ক্ষমা বিচাৰিবলৈ চেষ্টা কৰে, কিন্তু তেতিয়ালৈকে তাইৰ পৰিয়াল আন ঠাইলৈ গুচি গৈছিল। পিছত ৱাণ্ডাই এখন চিঠি লিখি সকলোকে ক্ষমা কৰি দিয়ে আৰু নিজৰ চিত্ৰ উপহাৰ হিচাপে পঠিয়ায়। এই কাহিনীয়ে ক্ষমা, দয়া আৰু সদাচৰণৰ শিক্ষা দিয়ে।

2 Mark Important Questions

Question 11 (2 Marks)

Why did Maddie feel responsible for Wanda's unhappiness?

Answer

Maddie felt responsible because she knew Wanda was being teased. Although she disliked it, she never tried to stop Peggy or defend Wanda.

Question 12 (2 Marks)

Why did Peggy and Maddie go to Wanda's house?

Answer

They went to Wanda's house to apologize for their behaviour and make peace with her.

Question 13 (2 Marks)

What did Wanda write in her letter?

Answer

Wanda wrote that she liked her new school and did not hate anyone. She also sent greetings to her classmates.

Question 14 (2 Marks)

How did Wanda show her kindness?

Answer

She forgave Peggy and Maddie and gifted them her beautiful drawings.

Question 15 (2 Marks)

Why was Maddie deeply affected by Wanda's departure?

Answer

Maddie realized that her silence had encouraged bullying. This made her feel very sorry.

Question 16 (2 Marks)

How did Peggy react after reading Wanda's letter?

Answer

Peggy felt relieved because Wanda was not angry with them and had forgiven them.

Question 17 (2 Marks)

Why did Maddie admire Wanda?

Answer

Maddie admired Wanda's talent, patience and forgiving nature.

Question 18 (2 Marks)

What lesson did Maddie learn?

Answer

She learned that remaining silent during injustice is also wrong.

Question 19 (2 Marks)

How did the girls feel after receiving Wanda's drawings?

Answer

They felt happy but also ashamed of their earlier behaviour.

Question 20 (2 Marks)

What message does the story give?

Answer

The story teaches kindness, understanding and respect for others.

3 Mark Important Questions

Question 21 (3 Marks)

Why did Maddie feel guilty after Wanda left?

Answer

Maddie knew that Wanda was being teased unfairly. Although she disagreed with it, she remained silent. After Wanda left, she regretted not helping her.

Question 22 (3 Marks)

How did Wanda prove that she was generous?

Answer

Wanda forgave Peggy and Maddie despite their behaviour. She even gifted them her beautiful drawings, showing her kindness.

Question 23 (3 Marks)

Describe Maddie's character.

Answer

Maddie was kind and sensitive. She understood Wanda's pain and later realized the importance of standing against bullying.

Question 24 (3 Marks)

Why is Wanda an admirable character?

Answer

Wanda was patient, talented and forgiving. She never took revenge and treated everyone with kindness.

Question 25 (3 Marks)

What is the central idea of the story?

Answer

The story highlights kindness, forgiveness and the need to oppose bullying and discrimination.
