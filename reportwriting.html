⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Universal Report Writing Formula

Important Note: Learn this format carefully. By changing only a few details, you can write reports on Independence Day, Republic Day, Tree Plantation, School Function, Science Exhibition, Saraswati Puja, Sports Week and many other topics.

Formula:

REPORT ON __________________

Place: ___________
Date: ___________

A __________ was organized/celebrated at __________ on __________.

The programme began with __________. A large number of students, teachers and guests participated in the event.

Various activities were organised during the programme. The participants took active part and enjoyed the event. The chief guest/principal delivered an inspiring speech on the importance of the occasion.

The programme ended with a vote of thanks. It was a grand success and was appreciated by everyone.

Reported by:
Your Name
Class X

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT REPORT WRITING

1. Report on Independence Day Celebration

Question: Write a report on the Independence Day Celebration held in your school.

Answer:

REPORT ON INDEPENDENCE DAY CELEBRATION

Place: Dhubri, Assam
Date: 15 August 2026

The 79th Independence Day was celebrated in our school with great enthusiasm on 15 August 2026. The programme began with the unfurling of the National Flag by the Principal. A large number of students, teachers and local people attended the programme. Patriotic songs, speeches and cultural programmes were presented by the students. The Principal delivered an inspiring speech on the sacrifices of our freedom fighters. Various competitions were also organised among the students. Sweets were distributed at the end of the programme. The programme ended with the National Anthem. It was a grand success and was appreciated by everyone.

Reported by:
Shakib Hassan
Class X
